Golf & Ski Upgrade

The Golf & Ski Upgrade adds coverage for participating in adventurous activities such as skiing. The upgrade also provides reimbursement up to $500 for lost golf or ski days due to severe weather and up to $600 for expenses to rent sporting equipment if equipment is delayed.
